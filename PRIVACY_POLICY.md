# Privacy Policy for Jan-AI-Chatbot

Last updated: July 21, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how this bot collects, uses, and protects information when you interact with it.

**Information We Collect**  
Our bot receives your public profile information (like your first name) and the messages you send to it.  
We do not collect any other personal data.

**How We Use Your Information**  
We use your first name only to personalize our responses to you (e.g., "Hello, [Your Name]").  
We use the content of your messages to understand your request and provide an appropriate automated response.

**Data Storage**  
We do not store your conversations or personal data.  
All interactions are processed in real-time and then discarded.

**Data Sharing**  
We do not share your data with any third parties.

**Contact Us**  
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the page admin.
